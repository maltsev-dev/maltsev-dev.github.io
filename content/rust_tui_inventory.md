### &emsp;&emsp;&emsp; Features
 1. Create and save new item 
    * product type
    * quantity
    * price per unit
 2. Show all items 
    * total price
    * sales tax
 3. Delete item by ID
 4. DB located at `const FILE_PATH: &str = "products.json";`
