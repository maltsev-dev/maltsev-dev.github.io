## 🎯 Philosophy

> **Sustainable Programming** — Writing code that stands the test of time through clarity, maintainability, and intentional design, **augmented by AI** to accelerate discovery and amplify human creativity.

I believe that great engineering isn't about clever tricks—it's about creating systems that are:
- **Understandable** — Clear enough that your future self will thank you
- **Maintainable** — Structured for evolution, not just initial delivery
- **Purposeful** — Every line serves a reason, every abstraction earns its place

As a **life-long learner and research-oriented engineer**, I constantly experiment with emerging technologies—from agentic AI to embedded Rust—and share what I discover.

## 🔧 What I Do

<div class="skills-grid">

### Embedded Systems & Microcontrollers
Developing firmware for ESP32, STM32, nRF52, RP2040 with a focus on reliability and real-time performance. I work close to the metal while maintaining code quality through Rust's safety guarantees and TinyML for on-device intelligence.

### Rust Development & Smart Contracts
Leveraging Rust's type system and memory safety for embedded, systems programming, and **smart‑contract development** (Solana, Polkadot). Safety and performance without compromise.

### Agentic AI & AI-Enhanced Development
Building autonomous agents that interact with systems, write code, and assist in R&D. I explore how AI can accelerate embedded development, testing, and simulation—turning ideas into prototypes faster.

### SDET & Test Automation
Advocating for robust testing strategies in firmware and backend systems. I design test harnesses, integration pipelines, and use property‑based testing to catch bugs before they ship.

### Game Development
Creating interactive experiences and simulations—from simple 2D games to physics‑based prototypes—as a creative outlet and a way to refine real‑time system skills.

### Full-Stack IoT
Complete IoT solutions: device firmware → wireless communication → backend services → user dashboards. Understanding the entire stack enables better architectural decisions.
