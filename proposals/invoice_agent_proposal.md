## Executive Summary

Your accounts payable team manually processes hundreds of supplier invoices monthly—entering data, matching to purchase orders, chasing discrepancies. Each invoice takes 10–30 minutes, errors slip through, and early payment discounts are often missed.

The Invoice Agent automates the entire process: ingesting invoices from any channel, extracting data with AI, matching to POs, flagging discrepancies, and initiating payment—all with full audit trails.

**What you get:** 80–90% straight-through invoice processing, 50% faster AP cycles, and your team focused on exceptions rather than data entry.

nvoice Agent is an automated invoice processor. It:

- **Receives invoices** from anywhere – email, EDI, scans, photos
- **Recognizes** any format (multi-OCR with backup)
- **Compares** with orders (2-way and 3-way matching)
- **Detects discrepancies** and highlights them
- **Creates payments** in your ERP
- **Manages audits** – who, when, and what was done

Integrates with 1C, SAP, Oracle, or any other system. Implementation – 6 weeks.

**ROI Snapshot:**
- **AP labor reduction:** 70–80% fewer hours
- **Error reduction:** 95% fewer payment mistakes
- **Early payment discounts:** Capture 90%+ of available discounts
- **Payback period:** 4–6 months

## Proposed Solution: Invoice Agent

### Core Capabilities

| Feature | Business Benefit |
|---------|------------------|
| **Multi-channel ingestion** | Email, supplier portals, uploads, EDI |
| **AI-powered OCR** | Extract line items, amounts, taxes, PO numbers |
| **PO matching** | 2-way (PO vs invoice) and 3-way (+ receipt) |
