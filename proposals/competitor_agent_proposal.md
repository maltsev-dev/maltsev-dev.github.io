## Executive Summary

You're losing sales when competitors undercut your prices—but you're also leaving margin on the table when you could charge more. Manual price checking is slow, inconsistent, and rarely happens at scale. By the time you react, the moment has passed.

The Competitor Agent monitors your competitors' pricing, stockouts, and new products daily—then recommends optimal prices based on your margin targets and competitive position.

**What you get:** Daily competitor intelligence across thousands of SKUs, automated pricing recommendations, and optional approval workflows that help you win on price without sacrificing margin.

Competitor Agent is your personal scout who monitors your competitors 24/7. It:

- **Monitors prices** daily for thousands of SKUs
- **Tracks availability** — stockouts, new arrivals
- **Discovers new products** on launch day
- **Analyzes promotions** — discounts, bundles, special offers
- **Makes recommendations** — raise, lower, or maintain your price
- **Integrates** with your pricing management system (autopilot is optional)

We set it up in 5 weeks. You select up to 10 competitors. We map SKUs and launch daily monitoring.

**ROI Snapshot:**

### Core Capabilities

| Feature | Business Benefit |

| **Stockout detection** | Know when competitors are out—raise your prices |
